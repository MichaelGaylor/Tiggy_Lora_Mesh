Your Farm. Connected.

Small, solar-powered nodes that talk to each other across your land — so you always know what's happening, wherever you are.

Up and running in three steps

No engineers. No wiring. No Wi-Fi needed.

1

Place the nodes

Put a small weatherproof unit at each spot you want to cover — barn, gate, trough, vehicle. Solar-powered, so no cables.

2

They connect themselves

Each node finds its neighbours and forms a network automatically. No setup, no passwords, no internet required.

3

See everything on your phone

Get alerts, check sensors, and control things from anywhere — on your phone, tablet, or computer.

What can it do for your farm?

Real problems. Simple solutions.

Know Where Your Animals Are

Attach small tags to livestock and get their location on your phone. Set up invisible boundaries — if animals leave an area, you get an instant alert. No more driving out just to check.

Protect Your Equipment

Know exactly where your tractors, trailers, and ATVs are at all times. Set up zones — get alerted the moment something moves when it shouldn't. Deters theft and speeds recovery.

Monitor Everything

Temperature in the lambing shed. Water level in the trough. Wind speed on the hill. All reported back to you automatically, with alerts if anything goes out of range.

Automate the Boring Stuff

Set timers to open gates at dawn, run pumps for an hour each morning, or turn on lights at dusk. Control it all from your phone — or let the system handle it on a schedule.

Talk to Your Team

Send short messages between nodes — phone to phone, across the farm — without needing mobile signal or Wi-Fi. Handy when you're in a dead spot and need to coordinate.

The network looks after itself

If a node goes down, messages find another way around. No single point of failure.

No Monthly Fees

No subscriptions, no SIM cards, no cloud services to pay for. The network runs on your land, under your control, forever.
